### Our lab will be on the second floor of the Biomedical Sciences and Physics Building at UC Merced when the building opens later this year. ### UC Merced can be reached: * #### by car: * Detailed directions to campus can be found [here](https://www.ucmerced.edu/maps-and-directions). * For visitors who drive to campus, UC Merced has [parking spaces](https://www.ucmerced.edu/parking) available for $8 a day. Visitor permits are for temporary parking in any non-specially marked space in the Bellevue lot. * #### by public transit: * From downtown Merced, you can take the "The Bus" along the [UC Merced Route](https://mercedthebus.com/220/UC---UC-Merced) to campus, or ride one of the UC Merced [CatTracks]() shuttles. * #### from out of town: * If you are visiting us from out of town and need to fly, there are several options. * The Merced Regional Airport [(MCE)](https://www.flymercedairport.com/) is closest, but has the most limited flights. There are also airports in Fresno [(FAT)](https://flyfresno.com/) and Stockton [(SCK)](https://flystockton.com/), which are both about one hour away by car. Additionally, for some travelers, it might be best to fly to San Francisco [(SFO)](https://www.flysfo.com/) or Sacramento [(SMF)](https://sacramento.aero/smf), which are each about two hours away by car. * If you like to travel by rail, there is an [Amtrack](https://www.amtrak.com/content/amtrak/en-us/stations/mcd.html) station in Merced. ### Once on campus, proceed to the Biomedical Sciences and Physics building. * The building is brand new, and doesn't appear on Google maps quite yet, but you can use the GPS coordinates in the map above to help guide you.
