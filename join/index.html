--- layout: pages title: Join group: join heading: bgimage: /images/slides/southeastern_US_dem.png --- # Join the Breitman Lab Our lab welcomes applicants from any race, ethnicity, religion, national origin, gender identity, gender expression, caregiver and family commitments, political affiliation, sexual orientation, and eligible age or disability status. Furthermore, we are committed to maintaining a supportive and collaborative lab environment. Read more about our lab compact and philosophy. ## Undergraduate Students We are always in the market for motivated and talented people, even if we do not have an open position currently advertised. We are interested in engaging with you in a conversation about your scientific background and goals for your future career (in academia, industry, or other ventures). We strive to provide excellent training across a wide range of computational and experimental techniques. If interested, please send your CV/Resume to [Dr. Flor Breitman](contact/). ## Postdoctoral Fellows Inquiries about Postdoctoral positions should be emailed directly to Dr. Breitman. We believe that people should be fairly compensated for their work, and so if we are able to help you work out a postdoctoral fellowship to work with us, we will do all we can to set postdoc compensation to reasonable rates, and we encourage candidates to explore applying for extramural fellowships to support their research. ## Other Visitors / High School Students We are also open to hosting other kinds of scientists who enrich our scientific environment and lab culture, including foreign exchange students, interns, and research specialists interested in collaborating, depending on project and funding availability. We are also open to working with and mentoring high school students; in fact, Dr. Breitman has experience with this. However, we cannot accommodate many high school students. Positions for high school students will be on a volunteer basis and will only be allowed for students that are the right fit. If interested please [contact us!](contact/)
